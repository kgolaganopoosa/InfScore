What's InfScore?

InfScore is a **simple, rapid influence calculator** designed for content creators, marketers, and individuals seeking a benchmark of their combined social media reach.

It aggregates the total number of followers across a comprehensive list of global and regional social media platforms (including TikTok, X, YouTube, WeChat, Douyin, and more). It then calculates a proprietary **InfScore** by applying a weight factor of **0.01** to the total follower count.

This score provides a quick, standardized metric to compare influence potential, offering insight into your digital footprint across the decentralized social web.
