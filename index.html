What's InfScore?

InfScore is a universal influence metric that measures your total social media reach across all major platforms worldwide.

We add up your followers from TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, WeChat, X (Twitter), and 20+ other platforms to give you one single number: your InfTotal.

Your InfScore is then calculated as InfTotal × 0.01 — a clean, comparable score that influencers, brands, and agencies use to quickly understand reach and influence power.

Perfect for creators, celebrities, marketers, and talent managers.
